Remote Patient Monitoring allows you to track your health from home, sending data to your healthcare provider. It means personalized care, fewer clinic visits, and better health outcomes.

It is recommended that you take a reading every day, ideally in the morning before eating or taking medications. Your healthcare provider will provide guidance on the specific timing and frequency based on your condition and treatment plan.

Yes, your data is encrypted and only accessed by authorized team members, meeting strict privacy standards.

Remote patient monitoring is a covered benefit under Medicare and Medicare Advantage, often resulting in minimal or no out-of-pocket expenses for participants in the Carestrides program. Monthly fees vary depending on factors such as meeting deductibles, usage patterns, and insurance coverage types. Carestrides' services are overseen by your healthcare provider, and billing for remote patient monitoring services is handled directly through your insurance provider.

Certainly! Carestrides is an extension of your primary care physician. You will still have regular appointments with your primary care physician, and each time you visit, your doctor will have access to all your essential Carestrides information.

Enrolling is simple - just have a conversation with your doctor. If you have a chronic condition, you might already qualify for Carestrides. During your next appointment, inquire with your doctor about the suitability of Carestrides for you, and then connect with one of our enrollment specialists to begin the process.
